# TC-DD-01 — Drag between gearslots

**Area:** DragDrop | **Client:** Era | **Mandatory:** yes

## Preconditions

- A gearbar with two compatible slots (e.g. both trinket slots), items equipped in both;
  bar unlocked or drag on slots enabled; out of combat

## Steps

1. Drag the item from the upper trinket gearslot
2. Drop it onto the lower trinket gearslot

## Expected

- The two items swap places (upper item now in lower slot and vice versa)
- Both gearslot icons update accordingly
- No item ends up on the cursor or in the bags unintentionally
- No Lua errors
