# TC-CM-01 — Hover lists eligible bag items

**Area:** ChangeMenu | **Client:** Era | **Mandatory:** yes

## Preconditions

- A gearbar with a trinket slot; at least two additional trinkets of uncommon (green) or
  better quality in the bags

## Steps

1. Hover the trinket gearslot
2. Observe the ChangeMenu that opens
3. Move the mouse off the slot and menu

## Expected

- The ChangeMenu opens adjacent to the slot and lists the eligible trinkets from the bags
- Hovering a ChangeMenu entry shows the item tooltip
- Items for other slot types are not listed
- The menu closes when the cursor leaves slot and menu
